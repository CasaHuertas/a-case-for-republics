A Case for Republics

The rate of inbreeding across the main houses of European dynasties

Yes, royals kept marrying their cousins to lock down power and keep the bloodline “pure.” In a republic, power rests with the people, so rulers can marry whomever they please, plus the odds of six-fingered offspring plummet. Plus you get free speech, minority protections, and the rest of democracy’s good stuff.

Something to ponder before we make monarchies kinda great again.
